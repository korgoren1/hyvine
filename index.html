💌 Since We Started Talking...

Dear Hyvine,

Since we started talking, there’s a quiet part of me that feels awakened. You’re in Mulot, I’m in Migori — yet somehow, your presence bridges the space between us more than you know.

But truth be told, this didn’t all begin with our first chat. The first time I ever saw you, we were at home — and you were heading toward Kataret Centre. You didn’t notice me that day... but I noticed you. I remember pausing for a moment, as if time decided to slow down just for me to take in your presence. That single moment stayed with me far longer than I expected.

I never imagined that the same girl I saw walking away that day would later be someone I think about so often — someone whose words make my day better, whose smile (even in imagination) brings light into my world.

We're not close in distance, but I hope one day, we can be close in something deeper — in laughter, understanding, and shared moments that aren't bound by location. I’m not here to rush anything. I just want a chance — a quiet, honest chance — to know your world.

And if you ever feel the same spark, even just a flicker — I’ll be here. Steady. Sincere. And so willing to try.

With a heart full of respect and hope,
Ezra 🌹
